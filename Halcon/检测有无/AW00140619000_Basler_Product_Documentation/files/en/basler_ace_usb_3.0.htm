Basler ace USB 3.0

Basler ace USB 3.0 cameras offer a USB 3.0 interface and comply with the USB3 Vision standard.

All Basler ace USB 3.0 cameras are equipped with separate input/output ports for triggered image acquisition or flash control.

The Basler ace USB 3.0 camera is available with several resolutions and speeds and with sensors from leading manufacturers.
