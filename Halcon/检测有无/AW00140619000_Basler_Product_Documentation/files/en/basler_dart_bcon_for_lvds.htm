Basler dart BCON for LVDS

Basler dart BCON for LVDS camera modules are developed specifically for embedded vision applications. They can be connected directly into an SoC or FPGA, allowing for the design of even the smallest of embedded vision systems.

Basler dart BCON for LVDS camera modules are available in the following variants:

  • Bare board: Consists of a circuit board only.
  • S-mount: Consists of a circuit board with a camera front attached. S-mount lenses can be attached to the lens mount on the camera front.
  • CS-mount: Consists of a circuit board with a camera front attached. CS-mount lenses can be attached to the lens mount on the camera front.
