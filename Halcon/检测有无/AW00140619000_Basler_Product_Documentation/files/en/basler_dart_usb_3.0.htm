Basler dart USB 3.0

Basler dart USB 3.0 camera modules are board level cameras designed for minimal space, low weight, and low power consumption.

They offer a USB 3.0 interface and comply with the USB3 Vision standard.

All Basler dart USB 3.0 camera modules are equipped with separate input/output ports for triggered image acquisition or flash control.

Basler dart USB 3.0 camera modules are available in the following variants:

  • Bare board: Consists of a circuit board only.
  • S-mount: Consists of a circuit board with a camera front attached. S-mount lenses can be attached to the lens mount on the camera front.
  • CS-mount: Consists of a circuit board with a camera front attached. CS-mount lenses can be attached to the lens mount on the camera front.
