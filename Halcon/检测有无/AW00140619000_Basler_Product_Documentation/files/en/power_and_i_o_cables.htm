Power and I/O Cables

Basler offers I/O cables as well as power and I/O cables of various lengths with open end.

High-quality power and I/O cables are essential for reliably supplying the camera with power and to avoid interference-free signal transmission to the camera.

All I/O cables offer flexible trigger capabilities and are EMI-tested. On top of that, the I/O PLC+ cables actively protect the camera against overvoltage as well as EMI and ESD.

To complete your cable assembly, the product portfolio also contains a variety of connectors.
