Mounting Instructions (ace USB 3.0)

This topic provides instructions on how to mount Basler ace classic USB 3.0,  Basler ace U USB 3.0, and Basler ace L USB 3.0 cameras in your system.

When mounting one of these cameras in your system, you must tighten the screws in a certain order. This ensures optimum alignment of the camera.

Depending on whether you use M2 or M3 screws, the order for tightening the screws varies.

  • Basler ace L USB 3.0 cameras use M3 screws of different lengths. These cameras can't be mounted using M2 screws.
  • The following illustrations show cameras with C-mounts. The mounting instructions apply, however, also to  cameras with CS-mounts.
