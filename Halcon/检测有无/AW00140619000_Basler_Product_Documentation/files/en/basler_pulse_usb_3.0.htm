Basler pulse USB 3.0

The Basler pulse USB 3.0 camera is a compact, low-weight camera with a metal housing and a tripod socket.

They offer a USB 3.0 interface and comply with the USB3 Vision standard.

The Basler pulse USB 3.0 camera is available with several resolutions and speeds and with sensors from leading manufacturers.
